Patient Monitor

Lightweight, convenient, practical, versatile, and easy to operate. Display six major interfaces: Non invasive blood pressure recording interface, standard interface, bedside monitoring interface, large font interface, dynamic trend interface, respiratory oxygenation chart interface. Adaptation parameters: Anesthesia gas detection, anesthesia depth, end tidal carbon dioxide, invasive blood pressure, touch screen, cardiac output CO, recorder.
