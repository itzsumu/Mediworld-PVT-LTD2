Anaesthesia Machine

Features:

  • Stable and Reliable: Meets the basic needs of anesthesia surgery.
  • 5.7" High-Definition LCD Display: Provides clear and detailed information.
  • High Precision Flow Control System: Ensures precise regulation of oxygen.
  • High Precision Vaporizer: Available in four types: Enflurane, Isoflurane, Sevoflurane, and Halothane.
  • Highly Integrated Breathing Circuit: Equipped with APL valve, providing excellent sealing and safety.

Technical Parameters:

Part of Anesthesia Machine

Component Description
Main Body High-strength engineering plastic rack, light, beautiful, and corrosion-resistant.
Scope of Application Adult, Child
Gas Source O2: 0.28~0.6MPa
Flow Meter O2: 0.05~1.0L/min, 1.1~10L/min
Flow Rate of Rapid Oxygen Supply 25~75L/min
Low Oxygen Pressure Alarm There will be a sound alarm when the oxygen pressure < 0.2MPa.
Vaporizer Automatic compensation based on pressure, temperature, and flow rate. Concentration range: 0~5 vol%. Choose from Halothane, Enflurane, Isoflurane, or Sevoflurane.
Respiratory Circuit Working mode: All-close, Semi-close, Semi-open. APL: ≤12.5 kPa.
Respiratory Bellows Bellows for adults and children. Tidal volume range: 0~1500 ml.

Part of Ventilator

Component Description
Display Mode High-definition 5.7" LCD screen display.
Ventilation Mode IPPV, MANUAL
Ventilation Parameters
  • Tidal volume: 50~1500 ml
  • Rate: 4~80 bpm
  • I:E: 2:1~1:4
  • Pressure range: 1.0~6.0 kPa
Parameters for Ventilation Monitoring Tidal volume, ventilation volume, IPPV rate, I/E, peak pressure of airway, pressure-time waveform.
Security Alarm System
  • Airway Pressure Alarm: Upper limit (1.0~6.0 kPa), Low limit (0.4~2.0 kPa)
  • Per-minute Ventilation Volume Alarm: Upper limit (1~20L/min), Low limit (0~19L/min)
  • Tidal Volume Alarm: Upper limit (0.5~2.0L), Low limit (0.02~1.0L)
  • Sustained High Pressure Alarm: Alarms when pressure > 25 cmH2O for 15s.
  • Suffocation Alarm: Alarms when pressure > 10 cmH2O and tidal volume < 120ml for 15s.
Power Supply AC 100~240V, 50/60Hz
