American Standard outlet

The American Standard Medical Gas Outlet is a high-quality, reliable solution for delivering essential medical gases in healthcare facilities. Designed to meet stringent safety and performance standards, this outlet ensures secure and efficient distribution of oxygen, medical air, and vacuum services, making it an indispensable component in hospitals, clinics, and emergency care settings.

Key Features:

Feature Description
Versatile Compatibility Supports multiple medical gas types, including Oxygen (O2), Medical Air, and Vacuum (Vac).
Safety Compliance Meets industry standards (UNE HD EN) for medical gas systems, ensuring safe and reliable operation.
Durable Construction Built to withstand the rigors of medical environments, providing long-lasting performance.
Easy Installation Designed for seamless integration into existing medical gas systems.

Applications:

Application Description
Hospitals and surgical centers Used for delivering medical gases during surgeries and patient care.
Intensive care units (ICUs) Essential for critical care patients requiring oxygen and other medical gases.
Emergency rooms and ambulances Provides reliable gas delivery in emergency and mobile medical settings.
