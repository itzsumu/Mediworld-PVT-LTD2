Oxygen Manifold

The gas manifold system displayed is a centralized distribution system designed to connect multiple gas sources (e.g., gas cylinders) to a common outlet. It allows for the safe and controlled delivery of gases to various applications or devices. The system minimizes manual handling of cylinders, regulates gas pressure, and ensures a consistent and uniform gas supply. It typically includes components such as connectors, pressure regulators, control valves, distribution piping, and safety devices like pressure relief valves.

Key Features:

Feature Description
Multiple Gas Connections Connects multiple gas sources, such as cylinders or bulk storage tanks, to a common outlet line.
Pressure Regulation Equipped with high-quality pressure regulators to reduce and control gas pressure to safe levels.
Safety Devices Features safety components such as pressure relief valves to protect against over-pressurization.
Consistent Gas Delivery Regulators ensure uniform gas delivery pressure across all distribution points.
Flow Control Includes manual or automated valves for precise gas flow control.
Material Compatibility Manufactured using corrosion-resistant materials like stainless steel for compatibility with various gases.
Expandability Can be customized and scaled to meet changing application requirements.
