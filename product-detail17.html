Medical Gas Control Zone Valve With Box

The CTFX-3A device represents a specialized medical gas manifold and valve assembly unit, designed for use in healthcare facilities. Its primary function is to provide controlled and reliable delivery of medical gases such as oxygen, air, or nitrous oxide. The assembly features pressure gauges and safety valves for each gas line, ensuring precise monitoring and operation. It is built to meet stringent healthcare standards like NFPA 99 and HTM 02-01, emphasizing safe use in critical areas such as Intensive Care Units (ICUs).

Key Features:

Feature Description
Multi-Gas Line Integration Supports simultaneous regulation and monitoring of multiple gas lines (oxygen, nitrous oxide, medical air, etc.).
Pressure Monitoring Equipped with pressure gauges on individual lines to ensure safe and continuous gas delivery.
Safety Valves Includes lockable line valves and pressure relief valves to prevent over-pressurization or gas leakage.
Durable Design Made with high-quality corrosion-resistant materials for long-term use.
Compact and Accessible Wall-mounted design allows for organized and efficient use in limited spaces like ICU and critical care units.
Compliance with Standards Adheres to medical gas standards such as NFPA 99, ISO 7396-1, and HTM 02-01 for safety and reliability in healthcare applications.

Applications:

Application Description
Critical Oxygen Delivery Ensures continuous oxygen supply for ICU patients requiring ventilators or high-flow oxygen support.
Emergency Response Allows easy access to regulate gas flow during critical emergency scenarios in intensive care.
Anesthetic Gas Supply Supplies nitrous oxide and other anesthetic gases for surgeries and sedations performed in ICU environments.
Gas Monitoring and Control Provides real-time status of gas delivery systems, ensuring uninterrupted respiratory support for critically ill patients.
