Oxygen Gas Alarm

The Medical Gas Alarm is a critical monitoring device designed to provide real-time alerts for the status of medical gas systems in healthcare facilities. This device monitors the supply pressure, flow rate, and overall functionality of gases like oxygen (O₂), vacuum (VAC), and medical air in hospital wards or ICUs. Equipped with high-sensitivity sensors and digital displays, the system alerts healthcare personnel to any deviations from predefined thresholds, ensuring patient safety and uninterrupted gas supply for critical care environments.

Key Features:

Feature Description
Digital Monitoring Features clear digital LED displays for real-time pressure monitoring of various medical gases like oxygen, vacuum, and air.
Multi-Gas Support Monitors multiple gases simultaneously, ideal for ICUs, operating rooms, and general hospital usage.
High-Precision Alarms Provides audio-visual alerts for low pressure, high pressure, or system malfunctions.
Fail-Safe Design Includes backup alarm systems to function during power outages or system failings.
Ease of Use Compact wall-mounted design with clear labeling for each gas type ensures easy installation and operation.
Customizable Thresholds Allows configuration of alert levels to suit specific hospital requirements.
Compliance with Safety Standards Adheres to NFPA 99 and ISO 7396-1 standards for medical gas safety and monitoring.

Applications:

Application Description
Continuous Gas Supply Monitoring Ensures uninterrupted oxygen and vacuum supply for patients on life support, ventilators, or other critical medical equipment.
Early Warning System Alerts staff of potential disruptions in gas flow or pressure, preventing delays in care during critical situations.
Vacuum System Monitoring Maintains proper suction levels for procedures like airway clearance or drainage for ICU patients.
Prioritizing Patient Safety Helps ICU staff quickly identify and address gas system issues, reducing the risks of adverse effects caused by pressure fluctuations or supply interruptions.
ICU Workflow Support Integrates with central gas supply systems to ensure maximum efficiency and operational reliability for critical care units.
