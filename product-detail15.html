Vaccum Suction

The Vacuum Suction is a high-performance medical tool designed for efficient fluid and debris removal during surgical and clinical procedures. With adjustable suction levels ranging from 200 to 1000 units, this device offers precise control and reliable performance, making it an essential component in operating rooms, dental clinics, and emergency care settings.

Key Features:

Feature Description
Adjustable Suction Power Provides customizable suction levels (200, 400, 600, 800, 1000) to suit various medical applications.
Precision Control Ensures accurate and efficient removal of fluids and debris, enhancing procedural outcomes.
Durable Construction Built to withstand the demands of medical environments, ensuring long-lasting reliability.
User-Friendly Design Easy to operate and maintain, reducing downtime and improving workflow efficiency.

Applications:

Application Description
Surgical Procedures Used for fluid and debris removal during surgeries to maintain a clear operating field.
Dental Treatments Essential for saliva and debris removal during dental procedures.
Wound Care and Drainage Helps in managing wound exudate and promoting healing.
Emergency and Trauma Care Used in emergency settings for quick and efficient fluid removal.
