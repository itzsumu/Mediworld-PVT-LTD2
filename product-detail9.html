400W Electrosurgical Unit Bipolar Diathermy Machine

Features:

  • It has independent endoscope output port and intelligent conversion key. After startup, it will automatically adjust to enter the endoscopic mode.
  • It has the memory function of power on and restart to restore the output record.
  • High definition large digital display.
  • It has automatic monitoring of power on and power off, automatic detection during operation and error prompt function.
  • It can work underwater and can be used with argon system, endoscope, hysteroscope, etc.
  • Equipped with the contact quality detection system of patient circuit electrode plate, which can detect the contact between the electrode plate circuit and the patient's skin in real time. Once the contact area is reduced and the resistance increases to the unsafe level, the machine will automatically stop output and give an alarm.
  • Panel control touch mode design - three channels of high-definition output power and large digital display - with different acousto-optic indications when outputting.
