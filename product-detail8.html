ECG Machine

The Neonatal Electrocardiograph is a state-of-the-art medical device designed specifically for monitoring the heart activity of newborns and infants. With a compact size of 23.9 cm, this portable and user-friendly ECG machine ensures accurate and reliable readings, making it an essential tool for neonatal care units and pediatric clinics.

Key Features:

Feature Description
Precision Monitoring Delivers high-quality ECG readings for accurate diagnosis and monitoring of neonatal heart conditions.
Compact Design Lightweight and portable, ideal for use in various clinical settings.
User-Friendly Interface Easy-to-use controls and clear display for seamless operation by healthcare professionals.
Durable Construction Built to withstand the demands of neonatal care environments.

Applications:

Application Description
Neonatal intensive care units (NICUs) Used for continuous monitoring of heart activity in critically ill newborns.
Pediatric clinics and hospitals Essential for diagnosing and monitoring heart conditions in infants and young children.
Emergency medical services Provides reliable heart monitoring in emergency and mobile medical settings.
